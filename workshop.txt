title=Barricade Hurt Zombies
description=[h1] Mod Content [/h1]
description=This mod makes zombies take damage from hitting barricade.
description=
description=[b] You can add and remove it safely from games [/b]
description=
description=[b] Mechanic[/b]
description=Each time a zombie inflict damage to a barricade it get hit in return.
description=
description=[b] Damage[/b]
description=With default damage, a zombie will take 1-2 wooden plank before dying. It can be customized in sandbox options
description=
description=[b] Barricade Type[/b]
description=There's 3 options available in sandbox settings.
description=[list]
description=[*] 1. Zombies receive damage from any player-built and player-moved object they thump on. (Default)
description=[*] 2. Zombies receive damage from thumping anything (e.g. Non player-built windows/doors, garage doors...)
description=[*] 3. Zombies receive damage from modded defenses-only (e.g. [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2927800433]Cheval de frise[/url])
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]To modders[/h1]
